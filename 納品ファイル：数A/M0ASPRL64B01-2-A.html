■ Quyidagi aniqlanmagan tenglamani qanoatlantiruvchi ( x , y ) butun sonlar juftligini Evklidning o'zaro bo'linish usulidan foydalanib, bittasini toping.
1) 143 x − 42 y = 1
x = 5 , y = 17

143 va 42 koeffitsientlari uchun Evklidning o'zaro bo'linish usulidan foydalansak,

143 = 42 × 3 + 17 42 = 17 × 2 + 8 17 = 8 × 2 + 1 Shunday qilib,
1 = 17 − 8 × 2 = 17 − ( 42 − 17 × 2 ) × 2 = 17 × 5 + 42 × ( − 2 ) = ( 143 − 42 × 3 ) × 5 + 42 × ( − 2 ) = 143 × 5 + 42 × ( − 17 ) 　......(1)

(1) dan, 143 × 5 − 42 × 17 = 1

Demak, izlanayotgan butun sonlar juftligidan biri: x = 5 , y = 17


2) 127 x + 38 y = 2
x = 6 , y = − 20

127 va 38 koeffitsientlari uchun Evklidning o'zaro bo'linish usulidan foydalansak,

127 = 38 × 3 + 13 38 = 13 × 2 + 12 13 = 12 × 1 + 1 Shunday qilib,
1 = 13 − 12 × 1 = 13 − ( 38 − 13 × 2 ) × 1 = 13 × 3 + 38 × ( − 1 ) = ( 127 − 38 × 3 ) × 3 + 38 × ( − 1 ) = 127 × 3 + 38 × ( − 10 ) 　......(1)

(1) ning ikki tomonini 2 ga ko'paytirsak,

127 × 6 + 38 × ( − 20 ) = 2

Demak, izlanayotgan butun sonlar juftligidan biri: x = 6 , y = − 20
