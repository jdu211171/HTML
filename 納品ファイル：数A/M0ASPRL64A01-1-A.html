■ 103 x + 45 y = 1 aniqlanmagan tenglamani qanoatlantiruvchi butun sonlar juftligi ( x , y ) ni Evklidning o'zaro bo'lish usulidan foydalanib bittasini toping.
x = 7 , y = − 16
103 va 45 koeffitsientlari uchun Evklidning o'zaro bo'lish usulidan foydalansak, 103 = 45 × 2 + 13 45 = 13 × 3 + 6 13 = 6 × 2 + 1 Shunday qilib, 1 = 13 − 6 × 2 = 13 − ( 45 − 13 × 3 ) × 2 = 13 × 7 + 45 × ( − 2 ) = ( 103 − 45 × 2 ) × 7 + 45 × ( − 2 ) = 103 × 7 + 45 × ( − 16 ) Demak, izlanayotgan butun sonlar juftligi biri, x = 7 , y = − 16
